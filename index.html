Tips for Freshmen

Tip #1

Don't use bottom floor Dwinelle bathrooms. It's a war zone.

Tip #2

Buy shower shoes. You'll need them.

Tip #3

Meet as many people as you can in the first few weeks.

Tip #4

Work in groups to help with motivation.

Tip #5

Don't be afraid to try new things. This is the best time.
